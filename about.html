About

I am a Full-Stack Senior Data Scientist and ML Engineer dedicated to bridging the gap between raw data and production-grade intelligence. I take full ownership of the data lifecycle, ensuring that complex models are not only accurate but also scalable, monitored, and well-architected. Driven by a fascination with sequential dynamics, I specialize in applying Markovian processes to real-world financial challenges—specifically time-series analysis and the optimization of investment portfolios.

My technical toolkit is anchored in Python and R, supported by a robust approach to Cloud-native architecture. I specialize in building scalable end-to-end pipelines using Vertex AI and Kubeflow, ensuring seamless transitions from experimentation to production. By leveraging Terraform for Infrastructure as Code (IaC) and implementing rigorous CI/CD workflows, I maintain high standards of reliability.
